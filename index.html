My name is Yeon

unpublished book.
I am a graphic design student and I was prompted to do a project about libraries. While investigating the ideas about libraries and publishing, I realized that I wanted to hear the unheard voices. I thought publishers should not be the only ones who decide what ideas should be heard and what should not.
So I invite you to send me your book that did not get published due to whatever reasons. I will design, print and hand-bind two copies of your book. One will be sent back to you and the other one will be donated to a library of your choice.
The Instruction is Simple:

1. Get a text file of your book.

2. Get a classification number according to Dewy Decimal Classification System.

3. Decide what library you want your book to be sent.

4. Send those with your address to yours.truly.book@gmail.com

5. Get your printed book.

6. Check out your book at the library.
